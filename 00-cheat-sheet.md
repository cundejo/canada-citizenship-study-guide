## Must-Know Government Facts

- **Head of State** = The Sovereign (King/Queen)
- **Head of Government** = The Prime Minister
- **Governor General** represents the Sovereign (5-year term)
- **Parliament** = Sovereign + Senate + House of Commons
- **Senators** are appointed (until age 75)
- **MPs** are elected in 308 ridings
- **Three levels of government:** Federal, Provincial/Territorial, Municipal
- A bill needs **Royal Assent** to become law
- **Majority government** = governing party holds more than half the seats

## Must-Know Rights & Responsibilities

### Four Fundamental Freedoms
1. Conscience and religion
2. Thought, belief, opinion, expression
3. Peaceful assembly
4. Association

### Key Responsibilities
- Obey the law
- Serve on a jury when called
- Vote in elections
- Help others / volunteer
- Protect the environment
- Defend Canada

## Quick-Fire Facts

- Canada = 2nd largest country (10 million km²)
- 3 oceans: Pacific, Atlantic, Arctic
- 10 provinces, 3 territories
- Population: ~34 million
- Two official languages: English and French
- New Brunswick = only officially bilingual province
- Aboriginal peoples: First Nations (65%), Métis (30%), Inuit (4%)
- "Inuit" means "the people"
- Hockey = national winter sport; Lacrosse = national summer sport
- Stanley Cup donated by Lord Stanley (1892)
- Largest trading partner: United States (75%+ of exports)
- 75%+ of Canadians work in service industries
- Supreme Court = highest court in Canada
- You are presumed innocent until proven guilty
